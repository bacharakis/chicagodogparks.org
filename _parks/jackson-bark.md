Jackson Bark is consistently ranked the #1 dog park in Chicago. Located in Jackson Park on the South Side, it features over 50 agility features — ramps, tunnels, weave poles, tire staircases, teeter-totters, and hoops — making it the most fully-equipped dog park in Illinois. Built with over 90% recycled materials and funded by the community, it offers both physical and mental stimulation for dogs of all sizes with free parking.
