Montrose Dog Beach is Chicago's crown jewel for dogs — nearly 4 acres of fenced beachfront on Lake Michigan where dogs can swim and play off-leash. It's the largest dog-friendly area in the city, managed by the dedicated volunteer group MonDog. A self-service dog wash station is available to rinse off after sandy, splashy adventures. Bring your own water as there are no fountains on the beach.
