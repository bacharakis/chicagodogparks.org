Wicker DFA, affectionately known as "Wicker Bark," underwent a major renovation in 2025 that expanded it by 25% and added new fencing, a shade structure, improved surfacing, and a second entrance. The $600K+ project was funded entirely by community volunteers through the Wicker Park Advisory Council.
