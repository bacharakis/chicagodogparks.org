Chicago Dog Parks is a community resource dedicated to helping dog owners find and enjoy the city's dog-friendly areas. We compile information about all 33+ official Dog Friendly Areas (DFAs), 2 dog beaches, and related community resources in one easy-to-use guide.

## What We Cover

- **Complete park listings** with addresses, amenities, and descriptions for every DFA in Chicago
- **Rules and permit information** so you know what's required before your first visit
- **Seasonal tips** to help you and your dog enjoy parks year-round
- **Community organizations** that maintain and improve Chicago's dog parks
- **News and updates** about new parks, renovations, and policy changes

## Our Sources

Information on this site is compiled from official sources including the Chicago Park District, the City of Chicago, local news outlets, and community organizations. We do our best to keep everything accurate and up-to-date.
