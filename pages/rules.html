DFA Permit

All Chicago Dog Friendly Areas (DFAs) require an annual permit for each dog.

🎫 Permit Details

  • Cost: $10 per dog per year
  • Valid: January 1 through December 31
  • Where to get it: 80+ participating veterinary offices across Chicagoland
  • No daily or visitor passes available

💉 Required Vaccinations

  • Rabies (current)
  • Distemper
  • Hepatitis
  • Parainfluenza
  • Parvovirus
  • Bordetella (kennel cough)
  • Leptospirosis

📋 Additional Requirements

  • Veterinary exam within the past year
  • Fecal test for internal parasites
  • Current City of Chicago dog license or proof of rabies vaccination
  • Completed permit application

City Dog Registration

All dogs over 4 months old in Chicago must be registered with the City Clerk's Office.

💰 Registration Fees

  • Sterilized: $5/year ($2.50 senior discount) or $50/3 years
  • Non-sterilized: $50/year ($5 senior discount) or $150/3 years
  • Requires proof of current rabies vaccination

📞 How to Register

  • Online, by mail, or in person
  • City Clerk's Office
  • Phone: 312-744-DOGS (3647)

Park Rules

🕐 Hours

  • 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM daily (7 days/week)
  • Exception: Bennett Park Dog Park is open 24 hours

✅ Required Behavior

  • Dogs must be leashed before entering and upon leaving
  • Gates must remain closed except during entry/exit
  • Owners must clean up immediately
  • Maximum 3 dogs per person
  • Owners are liable for all injuries caused by their dogs

🚫 Not Allowed

  • Dogs with dangerous behavior history
  • No food (human or dog) inside DFAs
  • Puppies under 4 months
  • Female dogs in heat
  • Children under 12 without an adult

⚠️ Fines & Enforcement

  • Failure to clean up: up to $500 fine
  • Enforced by Cook County Department of Animal and Rabies Control
  • Park Security: 312-747-2193
